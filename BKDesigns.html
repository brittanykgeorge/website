About Me

Founded in 2020, BK Designs & Custom Apparel is a female and Indigenous-owned company located on Secwepémc Territory in British Columbia. This online store is to showcase previously made streetwear and to connect with customers to put their ideas into a design on garments to be worn with pride – from our hands to yours. My vision is to fill the hole in the market that is missing the voices of Indigenous peoples. Support Local & Indigenous Entrepreneurs!
